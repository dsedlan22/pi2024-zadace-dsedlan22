## Domain description
The e-Menza application focuses on student eating experiences inside the SCVZ (Student Centre Zagreb) ecosystem. This system is designed to make ordering meals, offering feedback, and tracking dietary preferences easier in real time, through interactive and user-friendly interfaces. By connecting with existing student identity, e-Menza provides users with safe and individualized access, thereby improving their eating experience through technology. The application is designed to meet the demands of both students and SCVZ management, with features such as meal ordering, real-time menu updates, feedback gathering, reward programs, and thorough analytics for service improvement.

## Project Specification
The e-Menza software product is designed to meet the following key requirements:

- **User Authentication**: Integration with the CARNet system to ensure that students can securely log in with existing credentials.
- **Meal Ordering**: A user-friendly interface allowing students to order meals, with options for customization and dietary preferences.
- **Real-Time Menu Updates**: The application will display the most current menu items, including daily specials and item availability, with allergen information clearly indicated.
- **Feedback System**: A mechanism for students to submit their feedback on meals, which also contributes to a rewards program.
- **Order History**: A feature for students to view their past meal orders and feedback, offering insights into their dining habits.
- **Rewards Program**: A system rewarding students for engagement with the app, such as providing feedback and placing orders.
- **Management Dashboard**: For SCVZ administration, a backend feature to view and analyze aggregated data from orders and feedback to inform decision-making.
- **Performance**: The application must be responsive and reliable, particularly during peak dining times, to provide a seamless user experience.
- **Security and Privacy**: Strict adherence to data protection laws like GDPR, ensuring that all student information is handled with confidentiality and integrity.

Each of these requirements is crucial to provide a comprehensive digital solution that enhances the SCVZ dining experience while streamlining back-end management processes.
